Marvel's Spiderman is every spiderman fans dream. The web swinging and combat feel just as it should for a spiderman game. The storyline was okay but what really drives the game is the swinging and movement. I would genuinely just play this game to swing around and stop crime. There is so many spiderman games that have come out before this but it never really felt like a superhero game. Marvel's Spiderman on the other hand is a breath of fresh air making you feel like you are really flying between the streets of New York as your very own friendly neighbourhood superhero.

-Mark(Honest Game Reviewer) 1/04/2023
